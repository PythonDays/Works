For this activity you will create: A list of 10 questions that can be used during the user interview design process. Create a 10 question survey that can be used to collect data. Create a problem statement. Create a small presentation including mockups, sitemap, and personas from week 6 and the content created this week to display work.

Interview Questions:

    How often do you get your dog groomed?
    What are your primary concerns when choosing a dog grooming service?
    What features would you like to see in a dog grooming website?
    How important is the ease of booking appointments online?
    What would make you choose one dog grooming website over another?
    Have you ever had a negative experience with a dog grooming service?
    What would you consider to be a fair price for dog grooming services?
    What is your preferred method of payment for dog grooming services?
    Would you be willing to provide feedback on your experience with our dog grooming website?
    What do you think sets our dog grooming service apart from others in the industry?

Survey Questions:

    How often do you get your dog groomed?
    What factors do you consider when choosing a dog grooming service?
    Have you ever used an online platform to book a dog grooming appointment?
    How easy was the online booking process?
    How likely are you to recommend our dog grooming service to a friend?
    On a scale of 1-5, how satisfied were you with your last dog grooming experience?
    What do you think sets our dog grooming service apart from others in the industry?
    How important is the price when choosing a dog grooming service?
    Would you like to see any additional features on our dog grooming website?
    Would you be interested in signing up for a loyalty program for our dog grooming service?

Problem statement:

    As a dog owner, it can be challenging to find a reliable and convenient dog grooming service that meets your needs. Many services have limited availability, difficult booking processes, and inconsistent quality of care. We want to create a user-friendly website that makes it easy for dog owners to book grooming services online, while providing a high level of care and personalized attention to each dog. Our goal is to create a service that dog owners trust and feel confident leaving their furry friends with.
